DEPARTMENT OF NEUROLOGY

Neurology deals with the evaluation, diagnosis, and management of disorders of the brain, spinal cord, peripheral nerves, neuromuscular junction and muscles. As a healthcare provider dedicated to providing the finest possible care, the Department of Neurology at Jindal Hospitals provide comprehensive diagnostic and therapeutic Neurology services as per internationally accepted evidence-based protocols.
