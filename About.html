About Us

Who we are?
Transforming health care:

Jindal is the largest integrated, not-for-profit medical group practice in the world. We're building the future, one where the best possible
care is available to everyone — and more people can heal at home. Our relentless research turns into earlier diagnoses and new cures.
That's how we inspire hope in those who need it most.


People who make the difference

A diverse staff makes every single aspect of our organization better. At Jindal, we work to create the right environment for our staff to thrive,
feel valued, enjoy their work, and provide an unparalleled patient experience. Everyone at Jindal is on a mission to provide compassionate,
world-class care to each person, in every connection. That includes 1.3 million patients who visit Jindal campuses each year. We also train
future health leaders who share our drive to transform health care.

At Jindal Healthcare, our vision is to be the most well regarded healthcare provider in India committed to the highest standards of clinical
excellence and patient care, supported by latest technology and cutting edge research. We ask more of ourselves, so we can give more to
our patients. We push the boundaries of excellence in everything we do, so we can deliver the highest standards in patient-centred care.
