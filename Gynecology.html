DEPARTMENT OF GYNECOLOGY

With every stage of a woman’s life comes changes in her body and different health issues are faced at different stages. The Centre for Gynaecology at Jindal Hospitals offers world-class and comprehensive care for women. Our renowned specialists offer their expert opinion on preventive measures, ensure accurate diagnosis and provide suitable treatment. The team at the Centre for Obstetrics and Gynaecology is known to deal with the most complex disorders or diseases related to the female reproductive tract. Our doctors are highly experienced and skilled to cater to women of different age groups – from teenagers to senior citizens
